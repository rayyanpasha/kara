KARA: Empowering Urban Teens

Pitch Deck - Nebula Climate Action Fund

Problem Statement

Urban teens in cities like Bangalore face increasing dust and pollution due to rapid construction and metro projects. This impacts their health, confidence, and social engagement, while opportunities to act remain unclear.

Our Solution

  • Interactive platform connecting teens with local initiatives
  • Workshops and events to build awareness and leadership
  • Community projects to reduce environmental impact

Product Features

  • Dashboard to explore and join local environmental activities
  • Guided action plans for school and neighborhood projects
  • Rewards and recognition for participation

Market Opportunity

The target market includes urban teens in Bangalore (~1.2 million). Expanding to other cities could reach over 10 million teens facing similar environmental challenges.

Impact

  • Increased awareness of urban pollution issues
  • Higher engagement in actionable environmental projects
  • Community-level improvements in dust and pollution management

Implementation Timeline

  • Weeks 1-4: Platform development and onboarding schools
  • Weeks 5-10: Launch workshops and local events
  • Weeks 11-14: Measure impact and gather feedback
  • Week 15 onwards: Scale to more schools and neighborhoods
