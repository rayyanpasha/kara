The Crisis We're Solving

Bengaluru's teenagers navigate a daily commute through hazardous dust, which constitutes 57% of the city's PM10 pollution, chronically breaching national safety standards.

This crisis is enabled by a "growth-first" agenda and systemic neglect—evidenced by only 37% of clean air funds being used.

03

Our Solution

Kara solves the teen dilemma of "I want to act but don't know how"

Three Integrated Components:

  • AI-Powered App: Real-time AQI monitoring, AI-assisted complaint system, smart route suggestions, pollution hotspot mapping
  • "Dust Busting" Events: Community-led collection of hazardous dust with proper safety protocols
  • Circular Economy: Upcycling collected dust into sellable moss art frames, creating sustainable revenue

Current Traction: 600+ students engaged, 462 validated survey responses, 1 formal school partnership
