Bengaluru, India & Global Competitors

Bengaluru/India Competitors

  • Bengaluru Climate Action Clubs: BBMP-backed, school network for air/climate action, youth-led campaigns, local monitoring.
  • Alternative-24 / Vimove Foundation: Youth/teen engagement, school competitions, advocacy & event-based civic action.
  • Clarity Node Community: Community-led air monitoring, youth/citizen sensor data reporting in Bengaluru neighborhoods.
  • Filtair (Delhi): Teen-led DIY air purifier, education/workshops, expanding nationally, grassroots youth approach.
  • Earth5R: Pan-India citizen/youth “eco-action,” air quality micro-actions, school outreach.
  • Panjurli Labs/Chakr Innovation: Ed-tech, sensors, pollution upcycling, school partnerships (not always youth-led).
  • BCAP Youth Internship (BBMP): Municipal, youth-driven air quality mapping and city solutions.

Global Competitors

  • Cityzens4CleanAir: Youth-led wearable/sensor mapping, community air advocacy in African and global cities.
  • Climate Action Project & Climate Action Schools: Global school collaboration (146 countries), youth-designed projects for climate/air pollution.
  • IFMSA Youth Clean Air Campaign: International medical student network, youth education/activism for clean air.
  • FEE Eco-Schools: 59,000 schools worldwide, themed air/pollution curriculum, student-run environmental projects.
  • Sekolah Alam Pacitan (Indonesia), Sint-Paulus (Belgium): Global best-practice school labs, youth-led air/biodiversity innovation.
  • UrbanBetter, AIRTH: Youth sensor deployment, school outreach, air tech startups.

Summary

Kara competes with school-driven, civic-tech, grassroots youth initiatives and education-driven clean air programs on local and global scales—typically blending monitoring, advocacy, tech, and action.
