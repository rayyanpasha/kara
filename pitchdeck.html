The Problem Statement

CRISIS

Urban Youth Are Breathing Crisis-Level Air — And Feel Powerless to Act

The Environmental Crisis

  • 64-80 µg/m³ daily PM10 in Bengaluru (above 60 µg/m³ safe limit)
  • 51-55% of PM10 pollution from re-suspended road dust (preventable)
  • 19.4% of Bengaluru children have asthma/chronic respiratory symptoms
  • 25% increase in youth asthma hospitalizations since 2020

The Behavioral Gap

  • 84% of students use ZERO air protection (KARA survey, n=472)
  • 41% feel helpless about pollution despite wanting to act
  • Existing solutions focus on monitoring, NOT youth-led action

"I want to act on pollution, but don't know how." — Core Pain Point

Our Solution & Execution

KARA: Action-First Civic-Tech Ecosystem

1. AI-Powered Mobile App

  • Real-time AQI tracking + personalized health alerts
