Competitive Landscape

Bengaluru / India Competitors

  • Bengaluru Climate Action Clubs: BBMP-backed school network, monitoring focus, no circular economy
  • Alternative-24 / Vimove: Youth engagement via competitions, advocacy-heavy, limited tech
  • Clarity Node Community: Community air monitoring, sensor-focused, no youth action component
  • Filtair (Delhi): Teen-led DIY purifiers, education workshops, expanding nationally, no app platform
  • Earth5R: Pan-India eco-action, school outreach, broad focus (not air quality-specific)
  • Panjurli Labs/Chakr Innovation: Ed-tech + sensors, school partnerships, adult-led operations

Global Competitors

  • Cityzens4CleanAir: Youth-led wearable/sensor mapping in African cities, research-focused
  • Climate Action Project: Global school collaboration (146 countries), curriculum-based, no direct action
  • IFMSA Youth Clean Air: Medical student network, education/activism, no tech platform
  • FEE Eco-Schools: 59,000 schools worldwide, environmental curriculum, no pollution removal
  • UrbanBetter, AIRTH: Youth sensor deployment, data collection, limited community engagement

Summary: KARA's White Space

Competitors either monitor OR educate OR advocate. KARA does all three + physical removal + circular economy + revenue generation. We're the only teen-led, tech-enabled, action-first, self-sustaining air quality platform in India.
